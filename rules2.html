Learn How To Play

MISSION COMPLETE

(How to Win)

Your mission is to find and have the briefcase full of money in your possession when time runs out and the bombs go off.

The agent with the money is alive, rich, and the best we've ever seen!

MISSION OVERVIEW

Agents take turns going clockwise, playing cards from their hand. You must outsmart the others and rely on your memory as the briefcases change hands to take and keep the money, all while the timer steadily ticks down to zero.

MISSION PREP

(Setup)

The board and token are placed in the middle of the play area, and the action cards are shuffled.

Each player receives a briefcase that stays face-down the entirety of the mission...

and dealt 5 action cards to be their hand.

MISSION PHASES

(The Gameplay)

There are 3 phases to an agent's turn:
1. Turn or Burn
From your hand, play a card or burn any number of cards face-down instead.
2. Reload
Draw until you have 5 cards in your hand again.
3. Countdown
If the fuse is armed, the token moves one space closer to the bomb.
